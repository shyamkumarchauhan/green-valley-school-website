Admissions

We welcome parents and students to explore our admissions process and join our vibrant community.

How to Apply

  1. Complete the application form online or in person.
  2. Participate in our assessment process designed to understand each student's strengths.
  3. Attend an interview with faculty and admissions staff.
  4. Receive your admission decision and enrollment guidance.

Programs

Early Learning Center

For ages 3-5, focused on foundational skills and creative play.

Elementary School

Grades 1-5 with a balanced curriculum emphasizing literacy, numeracy, and social development.

Middle & High School

Grade 6-12 offering a rigorous academic program preparing students for college and careers.
